About us

Medicomforts established in the year 2019.The company has corporate office at Bangalore.Our Raw Material Suppliers are certified with ISO,CE and AAMI Standard.

Medicomforts is dealing in the manufacturing of disposable surgery packs,pre-designed drapes and wraps for health and organization.our main goal is to understand the dynamic of the markets and deliver the products accordingly.We provide sterilization report for all the batches.we ensure that all our products are customised as per the requirements of the clients and made within the consign time frame.

VISSION

Our vision is to deliver high quality innovative products manufactured under highly sterile condition.Our aim is to create a benchmark in healthcare industry by being one of the prime suppliers of surgical disposables.We are committed to give quality products at competitive prices which is one of our top priority.

VALUES
