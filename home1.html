About us

Medicomforts established in the year 2019.It is dealing in the manufacturing of disposable surgery packs,pre-designed drapes and wraps for health and organization. Our main goal is to understand the dynamic of the markets and deliver the products accordingly.We provide sterilization report for all the batches. We ensure that all our products are customised as per the requirement of the clients and made within the consign time frame.

The company has corporate office at Bangalore. Our Raw Material Suppliers are certified with ISO,CE and AAMI Standard.

VISSION

Our vision is to deliver high quality innovative products manufactured under highly sterile condition.Our aim is to create a benchmark in healthcare industry by being one of the prime suppliers of surgical disposables.We are committed to give quality products at competitive prices which is one of our top priority.

MISSION

What good is a smile if you’re nervous about it? Our practice was founded to help patients lead a stress-free lifestyle. The more confident you are, the less stressed your life is. There are many ways to fight anxiety; one is to be confident in your dental appearance. That’s where we come in. We make you confident in your smile.

VALUES

We develop strong teams, partnerships and relationships. We will engage, empower and train our employees in a safe and diverse environment. We are driven to improve our processes, technologies, and skills to innovate and continuously improve. We will strategically pursue new opportunities in the medical industry to sustain growth.
